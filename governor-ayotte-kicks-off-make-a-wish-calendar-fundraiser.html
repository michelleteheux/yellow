Governor Ayotte Kicks Off Make-A-Wish Calendar Fundraiser

Join us in supporting children with critical illnesses through our 2024 charity calendar initiative

Governor Launches Annual Charity Initiative

Governor Sarah Ayotte today announced the official kickoff of the 2024 Make-A-Wish Calendar Fundraiser, a statewide initiative to grant wishes for children battling critical illnesses. The event was held at the State Capitol with dozens of volunteers, past wish recipients, and community leaders in attendance.

"Every child deserves moments of joy and hope, especially when facing health challenges. This calendar fundraiser represents our community's commitment to bringing light into the lives of these brave children and their families."
- Governor Sarah Ayotte

The 2024 calendar features stunning photographs of New Hampshire's natural beauty alongside inspirational stories of wish recipients. Each month highlights a different child's journey and the wish that brought them hope during difficult treatments.

Fundraiser Goals and Impact

This year's goal is to raise $250,000, which would fund approximately 25 wishes for local children. Since its inception five years ago, the calendar fundraiser has granted over 100 wishes, providing transformative experiences for children and their families.

How to Participate

Calendars are available for a suggested donation of $25 and can be purchased online, at participating retailers statewide, or at Governor's Office events throughout the month. Businesses can also participate through bulk orders and corporate sponsorship programs.
