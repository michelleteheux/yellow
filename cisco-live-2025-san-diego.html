Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cancellation policy? +

Full refunds are available until 30 days before the event. Cancellations between 30-15 days before the event will receive a 50% refund. No refunds are available within 14 days of the event.

Is there a virtual attendance option? +

Yes, we offer a virtual pass that provides access to livestreamed keynotes and select sessions. Virtual attendees can also access recorded sessions for 90 days after the event.

What health and safety measures are in place? +

We are committed to providing a safe environment for all attendees. We will follow all local health guidelines and will communicate specific protocols closer to the event date.

Can I transfer my registration to someone else? +

Yes, registration transfers are allowed until 7 days before the event. Please contact our registration team to process a transfer.

What is included in the conference pass? +

The full conference pass includes access to all keynotes, breakout sessions, the World of Solutions exhibition, hands-on labs, networking events, conference materials, and daily lunch and refreshments.
